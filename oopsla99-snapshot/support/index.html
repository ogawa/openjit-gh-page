Mailing List

The follwing two mailing lists are available for OpenJIT:

openjit-announce@openjit.org
Announcements for the OpenJIT compiler system.
To join, please send a message with "subscribe openjit-announce" in its body, to Majordomo@openjit.org.
openjit-users@openjit.org
Public discussion list for the OpenJIT compiler system.
To join, please send a message with "subscribe openjit-users" in its body, to Majordomo@openjit.org.
