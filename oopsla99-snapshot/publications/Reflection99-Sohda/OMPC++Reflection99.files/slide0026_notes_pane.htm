This is a sample code of a OpenC++ metaclass for OMPC++.

This shows a part of TranslateAssign and one of the methods called from TranslateAssign. In TranslateAssign, the environemnt,  RHS, LHS expression, and an operator are received,  suitable transformation is performed, then the result is returned. Here, analysis and transformation are performed about RHS and LHS.
WriteExpressionAnalysis is the method which analysis and transform about write accessing. WriteStart and Writeend Statement are inserted before and after the statement accessed.
